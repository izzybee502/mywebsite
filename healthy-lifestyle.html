Healthy Lifestyle

Your mind and botter both matter, and you cannot be happy if your body and mental health is struggling.

Excersise:

  • You can do a sport
  • You can go for prayer walks or runs 
  • do pilates in the comfort of your home 

Any form of excersise is good because it releases endorphins and things that make you feel overall happy and clear minded.

Eating:

  • A balanced diet is good for you 
  • I am not here to tell you to only eat veggies and fruit , but have a balanced and moderated diet.
  • try eating healthy alternatives , your body will thank you 
  • DRINK WATER !! take people seriously when they say drink water, its very important.

Organisation:

  • You feel happiest when you are in a clean and organised environment. so CLEAN YOUR ROOM!!
  • Have a routine , it can differ between days, but have some sort of plan, even if it just your morning routine. 
  • GET ENOUGH SLEEP!! Sleep is a serious factor in your life , people tend to take  sleep pattern for graned but it is so important. 

Self Image:

  • Care about how you look, not too much that you stress about what everyone thinks , but if you are happy in your own skin you will be happy. 
  • Look presentable and be clean for yourself , take care of yourself .
  • Dont do it for anyone else , do it for yourself .
