Hygiene

Hygiene and feeling clan is one of the most important aspects of a healthy lifestyle.

Skincare:

  • If your skin is fine , use a simple spf
  • Having a skincare routine makes you feel clean and fresh
  • Use gentle products and dont use active Anti-Aging ingrediets
  • A simple moisturiser and serum and spf and cleanser is more than enough for our young skin.
  • Try keep it simple with minimal products, dont buy tyhem for the aesthetic, buy them because they work.
  • lastly, be consistent with your routine or you wont see results

Body care and hair care:

  • I personally wash my body more than once, with a bar soap and then with a liquid soap, using a light exfoliating sponge or loofa
  • Moisturise your body everyday and look after your body skin as much as you would look after your face.
  • Hair is a different story, you can occasionaly wash your hair by preferance , at least once a week , i wash mine on wednesdays , fridays and sundays , but that can change depending on my mood. 
  • Shampoo and condition your hair, sometimes use a hair mask or oil as a treat. 
  • make sure your hair is always brushed and being taken care of. 

Scent:

  • To me , smelling good is so important 
  • you need to use deoderant , I use a roll on and a spray after the roll on.
  • Perfume is optional but always appreciated, you feel good about yourself when you smell good and dont feel gross in your own body.
  • You can also use body sprays and things like that. 

Teeth:

  • everyone likes a pair of clean teeth
  • everyone should brush their teeth with toothpaste twice daily
  • Flossing is also very importnat, and with braces you can waterfloss
  • You have to brush your teeth befor you go to bed so you dont sleep with germs in your hot mouth overnight, and brush them as soon as you can when you wake up in the morning, to get rid of those overnight germs
  • Mouthwash is optional but an added freshness is always welcome in my oral hygiene routine
